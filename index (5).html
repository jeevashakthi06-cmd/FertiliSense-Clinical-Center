🧬
IVF
In Vitro Fertilisation
Eggs are fertilised outside the body and embryos are transferred to the uterus. Recommended for blocked tubes, low sperm count, or unexplained infertility.
⏱ 4–6 weeks✅ 40–50% success
💉
IUI
Intrauterine Insemination
Sperm is placed directly into the uterus around the time of ovulation. Less invasive than IVF, often a first-line treatment for mild infertility.
⏱ 1–2 weeks✅ 10–20% success
💊
Medications
Ovulation Induction
Medications like Clomid, Letrozole, or injectable gonadotropins stimulate follicle development and ovulation in women with irregular cycles.
⏱ 5–10 days✅ 20–30% success
🌿
Holistic
Lifestyle & Nutrition
Evidence-based dietary changes, supplements, stress reduction, and lifestyle modifications that improve fertility outcomes alongside medical treatment.
⏱ Ongoing✅ Complementary
🥚
Egg Preservation
Oocyte Cryopreservation
Freezing eggs for future use. Recommended for those who wish to delay pregnancy while preserving fertility potential. Ideal before cancer treatment.
⏱ 2–3 weeks✅ Age-dependent
🧪
PGT
Preimplantation Genetic Testing
Testing embryos for chromosomal abnormalities before transfer, improving success rates and reducing miscarriage risk for IVF cycles.
⏱ Add-on to IVF✅ +10–15% success

🧬 IVF Treatment Details
In Vitro Fertilisation (IVF)
IVF involves stimulating the ovaries to produce multiple eggs, retrieving them, fertilising with sperm in the laboratory, and transferring the best embryo(s) to the uterus.
40-50%
Success rate (age <35)
4-6 wks
Full cycle duration
Who is it for? Blocked fallopian tubes, male factor infertility, PCOS, unexplained infertility, recurrent miscarriage, genetic screening (PGT).
💉 IUI Treatment Details
Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)
A procedure where washed sperm is placed directly into the uterus around ovulation to increase chances of fertilisation. Often combined with ovulation-stimulating medications.
